Featured Game of the Month

Rogue Legacy

After beating this game 4 times over and still coming back for more makes this definitley one of my top games this year. That fact that you lose 95% of your money everytime you die really amps up critical thinking and decision making. You have to scavenge and play it safe while deciding if you should skip the room or see whats inside the chest. These constant decisions with reward or consequence make the game easy to pick up and put down. I think thats a compliment.
